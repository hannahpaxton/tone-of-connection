Explore Your Mood

Tone of Connection will give you a random prompt and ask you to provide your thoughts. It will analyze your post for tone qualities and you'll be presented with a tone result.

Bring Color to Your Results

The stronger a detected tone is in your results, the more saturated the corresponding color will be. A weakly detected tone will be represented with a lighter shade of the base mood color and a more pronounced tone will be more vibrant.

Save Your Spot on the Mood Map

Feeling blue? Maybe others are, too! With your given zip code, Tone of Connection will post your thoughts to the Mood Map with a colored marker corresponding to your most resonant tone.

Look Back at Your Personal Tone Journey

All analyzed posts will save to your journal where you'll have the chance to look your past posts and optionally filter by tone that was present in your results.

Meet Your Mood

Our analyzer, powered by IBM Watson, looks for the following emotional & language tones

Anger

An emotion characterized by tension and hostility arising from frustration, real or imagined injury by another, or perceived injustice

Joy

A feeling of extreme gladness, delight, or exultation of the spirit arising from a sense of well-being or satisfaction

Disgust

A feeling of revulsion or strong disapproval aroused by something unpleasant or offensive

Analytical

Relating to or using analysis or logical reasoning

Sadness

An emotional pain associated with feelings of disadvantage, loss, despair, grief, helplessness, disappointment and sorrow

Fear

An unpleasant emotion caused by the belief that someone or something is dangerous, likely to cause pain, or a threat

Confident

A feeling of self-assurance arising from one's appreciation of one's own abilities or qualities

Tentative

Indicating a lack of confidence or certainty
