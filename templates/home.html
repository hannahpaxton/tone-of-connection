How Tone of Connection Works

I'll give you a prompt and you provide me with your thoughts. I'll analyze your post for tone qualities and tell you what I detected. The stronger a detected tone is, the more saturated the corresponding color will be. If the tone is weakly detected, I'll lower the saturation.

With your given zip code, I'll post your thoughts to the Mood Map with a colored marker corresponding to your most resonant tone. Beyond your zip code, the Mood Map is completely anonymized and no personal information will be attached to your marker.
